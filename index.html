Color Official Game

Color game is a famous game in india where you can earn money. In this game, you predict colors by judging various patterns and win RESL money for every correct prediction. For example, you predict that green will appear and you bet 100 rupees, if green appears, you will get 200 rupees. In just 3 minutes, your money will double. A new color appears every 3 minutes. You can also lose money in this game, so there is a financial risk. Make sure you understand all market segments before investing and make sure you understand the privacy policy, disclaimer before investing.
